Why Cyber Hygiene Matters

Employee Perspective

  • Protects your personal and professional data from unauthorized access
  • Reduces risk of identity theft and financial fraud
  • Ensures compliance with company policies and industry regulations
  • Maintains productivity by preventing security incidents
  • Builds trust with customers and colleagues

Organizational Perspective

  • Prevents data breaches that can cost millions in damages and reputation loss
  • Protects sensitive customer and employee information from theft
  • Reduces vulnerability to ransomware and other cyber attacks
  • Ensures compliance with industry regulations and standards
  • Creates a security-aware culture that benefits the entire organization

About Cyber Hygiene Feedback Tool

Enhancing organizational cybersecurity through assessment and feedback

Our Mission

The Cyber Hygiene Feedback Tool was developed to bridge the gap between cybersecurity policies and actual employee practices. Our mission is to help organizations identify vulnerabilities in their human firewall and provide actionable insights to strengthen their overall security posture.

By combining employee behavior assessment with organizational policy evaluation, we provide a comprehensive view of your cyber hygiene that goes beyond traditional security audits.

How It Works

Assessment

Employees and administrators complete tailored questionnaires about security practices and policies.

Analysis

Our system evaluates responses against security benchmarks and identifies strengths and weaknesses.

Feedback

Detailed reports provide actionable recommendations for both individuals and the organization.

Key Benefits

Risk Reduction

Identify and address vulnerabilities before they're exploited by attackers.

Employee Awareness

Personalized feedback helps employees understand their security role.

Policy Alignment

Reveal gaps between security policies and actual employee practices.

Progress Tracking

Measure improvements over time with periodic assessments.
